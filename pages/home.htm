{{ 'The challenge'|_ }}

{{ 'The Doñana area has been facing increasing pressure on water resources and biodiversity for decades, driven by intensive agricultural activity and wider environmental pressures.'|_ }} {{ 'ReNatgro aims to integrate existing knowledge and demonstrate a holistic and scalable approach to sustainable farming.'|_ }}

{{ 'Water pressure'|_ }}
{{ 'Agricultural activity in the area places significant pressure on water resources, making sustainable management an urgent priority.'|_ }}
{{ 'Biodiversity loss'|_ }}

{{ 'Need for practical solutions'|_ }}
{{ 'There is a strong need for coordinated, scalable approaches that support both productive agriculture and ecological restoration.'|_ }}
